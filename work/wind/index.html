He Knows Where the Wind is from

A series of contemporary ink art

NYU Shanghai Gallery, Shanghai

May 13th, 2015

Wind is hard to tell, unless with the help of its surroundings. Wind is hard to record. You can barely find wind data of yesterday online. This series of ink art is trying to record and reproduce wind by how the ink spread in water.

I used an Arduino controlled fan to simulate the wind according to the 13-day data collected from weather.com.
