President Washington and the Hedgehog

Augmented reality physics-based mobile game

NYU Tandon, Brooklyn, New York

Dec 9th, 2016

You may know that George Washington got a hetchet from his father when he was young. Later, it came the story of cherry tree. However, do yo know what happened on his way to the cherry tree? He actually met a small hedgehog but at that moment he was too afraid to bring the hedgehog back home. President Washington has been looking for that hedgehog for a long time and now it is your turn to help the hedgehog back to Washington's home.

President Washington and the Hedgehog is an augmented reality game that you can play using your phone and a one dollar bill.

President Washington and the Hedgehog AR Game Documentation from Ada Zhao on Vimeo.

In order to create a game that achieves the mixed reality effect, I built all the 3D models on my own in Maya. Here is a fully rendered final movie.
