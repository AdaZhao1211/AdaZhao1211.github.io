Rehappy

A new design of physical rehabilitation

NYU Shanghai, Shanghai

May 11th, 2015

Alternative video link for people in China

This is a product we designed for Microsoft Design EXPO 2015. Inspired by Xbox, our group thought physical rehabilitation can be more interesting and engaging if we take it as a game controller. Based on this initial idea, we interviewed Kevin, a student from NYU Shanghai, who once broke his arm and had to rehab with a physical therapist. Kevin confirmed our assumption: the current physical rehabilitation is boring and tedious. With the electronic device attached to patients, we think this system can also provide more statistic information to therapists. A therapist from Nanjing expressed the general need of data when analyzing patient’s current state.
