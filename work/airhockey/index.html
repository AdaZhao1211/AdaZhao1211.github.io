Air Hockey

Two-player mobile game

NYU Shanghai, Shanghai

Nov 28th, 2015

Inspired by the air hockey table in the arcade, this game moved the table to your mobile phones. Two players can control the handles using their phones seperately and the ball will be bounced back and forth between two screens.

It was achieved using Node.js and socket.io
