Overpopulation

A web page that tracks the current world population

ITP, New York

Aug 16th, 2016

Impressed by what Dan Brown described in his latest novel, Inferno, I created this webpage to raise people's awareness of the related issues like the lack of water. This web page calls the world population API, counts how many people will there be tomorrow in a certain coountry and matchs it with that country on the map. You can also see your population rank in the world!
